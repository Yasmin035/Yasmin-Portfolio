Pasta with Beef Stew

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • Garlic, onions, tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, spices

Boil pasta until tender. Prepare beef stew with garlic, onions, and tomatoes. Serve hot together.
